Why Kerala Food is Special

Kerala cuisine is known for its bold flavors, extensive use of coconut, and a variety of spices. Each dish tells a story of tradition, culture, and the region's connection to nature.

Idiyappam with Egg curry

The steamed Idiappam, famous for its delicate texture, combined with the delicious broth of the traditionally cooked egg roast form a perfect match for one's taste buds.

Kerala Seafood

The coastal region of Kerala offers a wide variety of seafood dishes such as Karimeen Pollichathu, Chemmeen Curry, and Fish Moilee, known for their rich coconut-based sauces.

Puttu and Kadala Curry

Steamed rice flour and coconut, served with a delicious black chickpea curry, is a popular breakfast in Kerala.
